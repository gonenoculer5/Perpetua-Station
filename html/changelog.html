  • Added a new belt quiver to carry arrows.

  • New export prices for bows, arrows.

  • New variation of bone armor (heavy bone armor)
  • Cotton seeds in askwalker nest for bowcrafting.
  • QOL cultivator for ashwalkers added to nest.

  • New sounds for drawing the arrow and firing.
  • New weapon and ammo type for bows.
  • Added a crafting recipe for the primal explorers belt.
  • A new material Silk which can be made from cloth on the loom. (needed in bowcrafting)
  • Added Sprites for the new items and materials.
  • New sprites for existing bone equipment.
  • Cloth is now a subtype of cotton and coded in descending order of creation. crafting recipes are updated to reflect this. Cotton>cloth>silk
  • Crafting refactor
  • Monstermos from yogs, which significantly improves atmos performance.
  • Firedoors are no longer full-tile. This means you can walk though a pair of firedoors without depressurizing a room.
  • Space wind is faster and deadlier.
  • Windows are significantly stronger, with most having 4x as much health.
  • Windows bordering space now have firelock windows to seal breaches.
  • You can automatically walk through a closed firedoor if doing so isn't likely to kill you.
  • Did I mention atmos is faster? It's way faster. Gas will quickly flood a room.
  • Improved linux support for extools. This means nothing for players, but might be useful for downstream server owners. Some of the code was created by SpaceManiac.
  • Air alarms will turn the lights blue in areas with hazardous atmos.
  • Lavaland atmos has a higher minimum pressure.
  • Cycling airlocks that don't suck. Ported from Yogs.
  • Players can now easily construct cycling airlocks.
  • To make repressurizing airlocks faster, the default distro pressure has been increased to 500 kPa. Atmos Techs can increase repressurization speed further.
  • Lavaland scrubbers will scrub BZ and other harmful gasses by default.
  • Mechs can open firelocks in some cases. Credit to MacHac at yogs.
  • Crew transfer vote will occur at the 90 minute mark, and every 30 minutes thereafter.
